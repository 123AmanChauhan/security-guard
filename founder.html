FOUNDER DESK

First and foremost i would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to our respected and treasured clients, whose persistent support and confidence in NDS security services encourages us to extend the services and enables us to provide value for their assets and safety, in order to remain competitive and desirable to our existing and new customers.

Private security is essential to ensure the security and safety of persons and property, as well as sensitive corporate information. Along with security we stepped into housekeeping facility management as well. We strived hard to make a mark for ourselves in this arena too and because of this hard work only, NDS security services has made its presence felt in each and every nook of the country.

We believe in the fact, that first line staff is just like a back bone of any institution whether it is an industry or a corporate office. “services are not a product but process”. Hence the role of a peon, housekeeping boy, receptionist or even a security guard is extremely important to make this process a huge success. A well trained security guard adds to the glory of the company. Similarly, if a well trained housekeeping boy performs his work in a rightful manner then definitely it plays its responsibility in adding to the brilliance of the company.

NDS security services is reluctant to provide a total services solution under one roof, we provide the well trained staff in all manners and try our to stand by our words. I believe a well-established agency with a trimmed, motive and trained staff will go a long way in assuring its clients a good night’s sleep.

I therefore , seek continued patronage of our valued customers, cooperation of our employees and thank our well wishers who have contributed to the growth of our organization.
Thanks and warm regards
Jai Hind !

Sudesh Kumar Panwar
Managing Director
