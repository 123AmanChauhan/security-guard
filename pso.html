PSO SERVICES

NDS Security Services Private Limited has achieved a reputation in providing professional Personal Security Officers in Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida and NCR. Our PSO in Delhi, Gurgaon, Noida and NCR offers reliable executive celebrity and VIP protection...

Everytime Secure Services was created with a vision to provide Executive Protection Services of International Standards to Indian Celebrities, Politicians, Business Tycoons and Foreign Delegates visiting India for business or leisure purposes. We hand pick people retired from various defence services (ex-servicemen) like Army, BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, State Police services etc. and train them to handle Executive Protection Services in civil life. For special assignments, we also hire NSG Commandos, Special Protection Group (SPG) Officers, Marine Commandos (Marcos) and Retired Senior defence Officers.

Our Executive Protection Team has protected CEOs of Multinational Companies while visiting India for business purposes, Dealer Meets for various companies on India Leisure Trips, Best Buyers Groups on India leisure trips, Hollywood Celebrities for Film Shooting and India Visit, Bollywood Celebrities for Film Shootings and Press Meet, Store Openings, Promotional and Social Events etc.

We are ETS, a security agency that caters to the unique needs of executives, VIPs, and celebrities.

With time, we have learnt that not a lot of people are particularly fond of the “Mob” effect that they get with executive security protection where the protected person is smothered and covered. Good protection work is the one that is always present but seldom seen. At ETS, we have highly trained agents who are experienced in working with the VIPs and celebrities, taking into account their unique lifestyle and specific needs. Our security services enable the person we’re protecting to live their lifestyle normally and we will adjust according to your needs.
