NDS Security Services Private Limited Training Center-Security Training Center

Aim & Objectives :

The Aim of NDS Security Services Private Limited Training Center is to provide quality training with special emphasis on practical aspects of security, safety & hospitality. With a aim that the trainee will emerge as a true professional to handle all the situations, related to security and or hospitality to become a responsible citizen serving the nation.

Following are the objectives of the institute :

  1. To create job to the needy youth hailing from the rural areas.
  2. To create useful and trained professionals in the field of security and hospitality.
  3. To provide well trained, dedicated sincere and responsible professionals to the corporate and the industrial world.

Training

  1. General Orientation
  2. Purpose and principles of Industrial security, security measures housing event vigilance and intelligence.
  3. Organization of security department, controlling mobs strictly, union agitation, demonstration, stoppage of work and labour unrest.
  4. Physical Fitness, Drill Discipline.
  5. Fire fighting, Fire Safety, First Aids.
  6. Identification of improvised Explosive Device and small Arms, Grenade etc.
  7. SAS (Security Automation Systems)and computer literacy programmers.
  8. Knowledge of Indian Penal Code, badges of ranks in police and forces. Use of Security equipments.
